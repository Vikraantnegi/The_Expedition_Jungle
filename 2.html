Deal of the Week!

Chandrashila Trek

Chandrashila is summit of the Tungnath, the highest Shiv temple in the world, located at a height of about 4,000 metres (13,000 ft) above sea level. This peak provides views of the Himalayas, including Nandadevi, Trisul, Kedar Peak, Bandarpunch and Chaukhamba peaks. Chandrashila treks are one of the most popular treks among Indian trekking enthusiasts. The trek to the peak is 5 km, a steep trek.
